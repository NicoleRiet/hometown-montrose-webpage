Visit Montrose

Enjoy cozy cafes, local boutiques, and a Jacaranda Tree-lined main street in this small town just 15 miles North of Downtown Los Angeles.

Top activities to do in Montrose, CA

Grab a nice breakfast

Cafe Montrose offers delicious breakfasts, lunches, and espresso drinks.

Coffee flights

Love all types of coffee? Try a coffee flight at Toasted Cafe!

Get lost in books

Lost Book Store has a huge selection of books and has plants for sale too!

Local beers on tap

A great tab list that rotates frequently so you can try a variety of brewskis.

Harvest Market on Sundays

This farmer's market is every Sunday from 8am-1pm. Find great produce, flowers, cheeses, baked goods, and more!

Local events

Wine walks, crafts fairs, parades...there are so many community events!

Local Guide

"Montrose is full of charm! You can spend a day grabbing a coffee at one of the many cafes, go shopping, and finish the night at one of the great restaurants or bars!" - Nicole, resident
